Book Review

Construction Planning, Equipment, and Methods

Focus: Construction planning, equipment selection, productivity, and project management

Overview: Construction Planning, Equipment, and Methods is a comprehensive textbook widely used in civil engineering and construction management. It provides a detailed understanding of how construction projects are planned, executed, and managed using modern equipment, methods, and industry practices.

The 10th edition incorporates updated real-world case studies, modern construction technologies, and improved explanations of key topics such as equipment selection, productivity analysis, and project planning. The book emphasizes practical applications, making it highly relevant for both academic learning and professional practice.

This resource supports the development of essential skills in construction management, including planning, decision-making, and resource optimization, which are critical in today’s evolving AEC industry.

The concepts presented in this book also provide a strong foundation for integrating AI and data-driven tools into construction planning and decision-making processes.
