About Me

Hello, I am Su Myat Thiri Tun. As an Electrical Design Engineer and Teaching Assistant, I focus on integrating intelligent technologies with physical engineering, design, and organizational safety. This portfolio consolidates my projects, literature reviews, and weekly AI article digests for the semester, showcasing how artificial intelligence augments architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) workflows.
